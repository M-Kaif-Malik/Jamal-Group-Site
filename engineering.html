Our Legacy

Respected Industrial Engineering Partner

Jamal Engineering Works (Pvt) Ltd is one of the oldest and most respected industrial enterprises within the Jamal Group, proudly serving Pakistan's engineering and manufacturing sector for over 35 years. Since its establishment, the company has been built on a commitment to quality, precision, and reliability, earning the trust of customers and industry partners across the country.

We specialize in the manufacturing of high-quality automobile components for two-wheelers and three-wheelers, supplying parts to multiple leading companies throughout Pakistan. Through decades of technical expertise and continuous improvement, Jamal Engineering Works has developed a strong reputation as a dependable vendor capable of meeting demanding quality standards and production requirements.

Fabrication & Contracting

In addition to automotive component manufacturing, the company provides comprehensive steel fabrication and engineering contracting services, delivering customized solutions for industrial, commercial, and infrastructure projects. Our experienced team, modern manufacturing capabilities, and customer-focused approach enable us to execute projects efficiently while maintaining the highest standards of workmanship and safety.

As the founding pillar of the Jamal Group, Jamal Engineering Works continues to uphold the values that have defined our success for more than three decades: integrity, innovation, quality, and long-term partnerships.
