About The Subsidiary

Civil contracting with corporate precision.

Jamal Construction Works (SMC) Pvt Ltd is a leading construction and infrastructure development company dedicated to delivering high-quality civil engineering and contracting solutions across Pakistan. As a proud member of the Jamal Group, we combine technical expertise, professional management, and a commitment to excellence to successfully execute projects that contribute to the nation's growth and development.

At Jamal Construction Works, we are committed to delivering projects on time, within budget, and to the highest standards of quality and safety. Our team of experienced engineers, project managers, and technical professionals ensures that every project is executed with precision, transparency, and attention to detail.

Mission & Strategy

Driven by integrity, innovation, and customer satisfaction, we continue to build lasting partnerships with government institutions, private organizations, and stakeholders. Our mission is to create sustainable infrastructure that improves communities, supports economic growth, and leaves a lasting positive impact for future generations.

Our Scope

Proven civil capability across major project types.

Roads & Highways

Construction of arterial networks, overlay paving, and asphalt lanes built for municipal traffic.

Drainage Systems

Installation of precast culverts, open channel conduits, and urban stormwater drainage schemes.

Public Buildings

Structural contracting, concrete framing, and custom civil shells for public utilities.

Urban Infrastructure

General contracting for public gardens, greenbelt structures, and city plaza enhancements.

Utility Works

Heavy civil excavation for main water supply links, trunk sewers, and electrical utility corridors.

Site Development

Land clearing, bulk excavation, compaction, and grading for housing and industrial schemes.
