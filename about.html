Company profile

Building a legacy of trust, innovation and scale.

Jamal Group has grown from a single enterprise into a diversified industrial holding with deep expertise in construction, engineering and concrete manufacturing.

Our history

Three decades of steady growth and industrial impact.

Since the founding of Jamal Group, the organization has pursued strategic expansion across Pakistan's built environment and manufacturing sectors. The group has earned a reputation for reliability, disciplined execution and long-term client relationships.

1990

Founding of Jamal Construction Works

Started as a road and infrastructure contractor supported by local government projects.

2005

Launch of Jamal Engineering Works

Expanded into precision fabrication and components manufacturing for the automotive sector.

2015

Established Jamal Concrete Solutions

Opened a modern concrete manufacturing facility to supply paving, blocks and precast products.

2026

National operations and renewed vision

Operating nationwide across multiple sectors with a renewed focus on partnerships and industrial quality standards.
