Company Profile

A Legacy of Trust, Precision, and Scale.

Jamal Group has grown from a premier engineering workshop into a leading diversified industrial group with deep expertise in manufacturing, construction, and concrete products.

Our Timeline

Three decades of strategic growth and development.

Jamal Group is a growing business group dedicated to delivering excellence in manufacturing, engineering, and construction. Built on a foundation of quality, integrity, and innovation, the Group has established a strong reputation for providing reliable products and services that support Pakistan’s industrial and infrastructure development.

Today, the group operates across three main divisions, providing specialized services and building materials to private developers and public agencies alike.

1995

Founding of Jamal Engineering Works (Pvt) Ltd

Established as the founding pillar of the group, focusing on precision automobile component manufacturing, mechanical assemblies, and custom steel fabrications.

2019

Establishment of Jamal Construction Works (SMC) Pvt Ltd

Formed to deliver public infrastructure projects including roadways, complex drainage systems, utility works, and public structures for prominent urban authorities.

2025

Launch of Jamal Concrete Solutions (Pvt) Ltd

Opened a modern manufacturing facility producing paving stones, kerbstones, hollow blocks, and specialized structural precast elements.
